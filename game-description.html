MazeQuest

How To Play:

Choose a player.

Find your way out of the maze using the least amount of steps (1 player) or before your opponent (2 players).

Player 1: use (W) to go up, (A) to go left, (D) to go right, (S) to go down.

Player 2: use (I) to go up, (J) to go left, (L) to go right, (K) to go down.
